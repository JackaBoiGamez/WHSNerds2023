The Team
Engineering

The engineering team is in charge of
the design, build, and test of the robot.
They are also in charge of the build of
the team exhibit.

Business

The business team is responsible for the
marketing of our company and our product.

Greenpower

The Greenpower team is a dynamic
STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering,
and Mathematics) initiative that
encompasses a wide variety of skills.
The goal of this program is to
expose students to the world of engineering
and innovation.

Technology

The technology team handles computer and
other tech related activities having to do
with the robot, website, computer aided
design, video, and events. Some of these
events are Cyberpatriot competitions.
