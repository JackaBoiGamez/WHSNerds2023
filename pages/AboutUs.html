About Us

In 2005, under the supervision of our CEO Dr. Vilardi, the Wetumpka High School Nerds was
created with a mission statement to establish a STEM culture in our community while
empowering our team members to become competent, confident, and self-directed problem solvers.

This year, our company has 40 members. Of those 40 members, 9 are female, 31 of them are male.
Our company is divided into three different divisions, Business, Engineering, and Technology,
with a CEO over the entire company being Dr. Virginia Vilardi. Each main division
has a team leader with a mentee who can lead the team to success in the future.
